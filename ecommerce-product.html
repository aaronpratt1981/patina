The Alpine Shell Jacket is the product of years spent refining what it means to move light and move fast through mountain terrain. The outer face fabric — a 70D nylon ripstop — resists abrasion without adding bulk, while a durable water-repellent finish sheds everything from fine mist to sustained downpours. Fully taped seams and a laminated storm flap behind the main zip ensure there's no path for water to find.

Inside, a clean drop-hem and underarm gussets allow a full range of motion whether you're swinging an ice axe or threading a ridgeline trail. The helmet-compatible hood stows away into the collar when not needed, and adjusts with one hand when conditions change. Two chest pockets, a Napoleon pocket, and two interior mesh pockets keep your essentials organised and dry throughout the day.
