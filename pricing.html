Frequently asked questions

Can I switch plans anytime?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated. Downgrades take effect at the end of your billing cycle.
Is there a free trial for Pro?
Yes, all Pro plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start. You'll only be billed if you choose to continue after the trial period.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and bank transfers for Enterprise plans. All payments are processed securely via Stripe.
Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or education?
Yes! We offer 50% off for verified nonprofits and educational institutions. Contact our support team with your verification documents to apply.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
Your data remains accessible for 30 days after cancellation. We provide a full export tool so you can download everything before your account closes.
