Modal / Dialog

Overlay dialogs for confirmations and form interactions. Focus is trapped while open.

When to use / avoid
When to use
  • Blocking confirmations and focused form inputs
Avoid when
  • Content is supplementary — use side sheet
<div class="modal-overlay" id="confirmModal"
  role="dialog" aria-modal="true"
  aria-labelledby="confirmTitle">
  <div class="modal">
    <h2 class="modal-title" id="confirmTitle">Delete project?</h2>
    <p class="modal-desc">This action cannot be undone.</p>
    <div class="modal-actions">
      <button class="btn btn-secondary" onclick="closeModal('confirmModal')">Cancel</button>
      <button class="btn btn-danger">Delete</button>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

Bottom Sheet

Mobile-friendly surface that slides up from the bottom of the viewport.

When to use / avoid
When to use
  • Detail views and editing flows on mobile/desktop
Avoid when
  • Simple confirmations — use modal
<!-- Trigger -->
<button class="btn btn-secondary" onclick="openBottomSheet()">Share item</button>

<!-- Sheet (outside .layout) -->
<div class="modal-overlay" id="bottomSheetOverlay" onclick="closeBottomSheet()"></div>
<div class="bottom-sheet" id="bottomSheet" role="dialog" aria-modal="true">
  <div class="bottom-sheet-handle"></div>
  <!-- content -->
</div>

Side Sheet

A panel that slides in from the right for detail views and editing flows.

When to use / avoid
When to use
  • Detail views and editing flows on mobile/desktop
Avoid when
  • Simple confirmations — use modal
<!-- Trigger -->
<button class="btn btn-secondary" onclick="openSideSheet()">Open side sheet</button>

<!-- Sheet (outside .layout) -->
<div class="modal-overlay" id="sideSheetOverlay" onclick="closeSideSheet()"></div>
<div class="side-sheet" id="sideSheet" role="dialog" aria-modal="true">
  <div class="side-sheet-header">
    <span>Edit item</span>
    <button class="btn btn-ghost btn-icon" onclick="closeSideSheet()" aria-label="Close">...</button>
  </div>
  <!-- form content -->
</div>
